Your work is important. Where you do it matters. We're here to help you find your next perfect work/study spot to assist you in your work flow. Whether you already know what you want or you're still figuring out what you like, introspotive is here to help enhance your experience and make it easier for you.

What is Introspotive?

Introspotive is a platform that aims to make it easier for you to find the perfect places to do your work, study, read, write, etc. From cafes, coffee shops, bakeries, libraries, and more, we wanted to provide a resource that makes finding the best place to work or do any other kind of activity of the like an easier process to get you out there.

Your productivity relies on your environment, and you deserve to find the places that works best for you. If you have preferences, that's perfect! And if not, that's perfect too! Our purpose is to help you discover something new, whether it be a new spot or what you like in your work environments.

Our Features

Reviews

Don't know what to expect from a place? Other users on Introspotive can pitch in their own reviews on the spots they go to so you know what to expect. There's nothing better than hearing from people just like you, and you can pitch in and leave reviews too!

Spot Specs

We made it easier to identify the key attributes needed in our work environments. From outlets, Wifi access, business, space, aesthetics, and more, there are many specific categorizations to help identify the space that best interests you.

Community Blog

Want to read or write an in-depth review about a specific spot and your experience? The community blog allows you to see where others in your community are going and is also a great space to share study tips, work resources, recommendations, and more!
