Introduction

Based on my current interests and the potential of my future abilities, my dream job would be related to something in design. I really enjoy creating graphic images/posters, designing websites, creative video editing, and much more that incorporate designing things and require a visual eye. Since I like a lot of different types of design, my dream job would be working as an in-house designer for a company. By being an in-house designer, I can have variety in terms of the type of design work I'm doing. It would probably be less stressful since I wouldn't be working with outside clients, and instead know what to expect when you're consistently working with one brand identity.
